À propos

CoreAccess is a technological project whose mission is to collect, structure, and enhance various data across several innovative fields such as renewable energy, space satellites, applied AI, and more. It aims to feed artificial intelligence and quantum learning models in order to anticipate future applications such as space tourism, new energy solutions, and climate research.
